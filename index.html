HOW TO REACH VJ GENTS

From Ernakulam Junction (South) — two convenient bus routes:

Route A (via Kaloor)

  • Walk from Ernakulam Junction to KSRTC Bus Stand.
  • Take a bus to Kaloor.
  • From Kaloor, board a bus to Olimugal Masjid.
  • Hostel is near the masjid.

Route B (direct)

  • From Ernakulam Junction, walk to South Ernakulam bus stop.
  • Direct bus to Olimugal Masjid (approx. 25 min).
  • Hostel is steps from the masjid.

📍 Olimugal Masjid is the landmark — hostel is adjacent.
